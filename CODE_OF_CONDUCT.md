# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Shaperail is an open source framework project. We expect contributors and
maintainers to keep the project technically rigorous and personally respectful.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a healthy community include:

- giving direct technical feedback without insults
- assuming mistakes before bad intent
- staying focused on the code, docs, benchmarks, and user experience
- accepting correction when a claim or implementation is wrong
- making room for contributors with different levels of experience

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- bad-faith arguments or deliberate derailment
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated low-signal disruption after maintainer direction

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in the repository, issues, pull requests, wiki,
and other project-managed spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or
block participants who violate this policy.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact the repository owner privately through the email or
contact method listed on their GitHub profile instead of opening a public issue.
